About Us

Welcome to KNK Group

KnK Group was established in 2010 with a vision to develop state-of-the-art projects. Starting with KnK Developers, the group has completed many multi-storeyed buildings and is now a trusted partner in the construction industry.

KnK Group has now moved into the industrial sector with KnK Industrial Contractors , offering exceptional industrial contracting services. Our team of experienced professionals includes architects, engineers, project managers, and construction specialists who prioritize safety and use the latest technologies to complete projects on time and within budget.

KnK Industrial Contractors are committed to maintaining a safe and healthy work environment while delivering high-quality, efficient facilities that meet the unique needs of the industrial sector.

We are proud of our track record of successful projects and satisfied clients, and we look forward to continuing to build on that legacy in the years to come.
