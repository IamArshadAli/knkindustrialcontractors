Pressure Vessels Repair & Alterations

Pressure vessels are an essential component in many industrial operations, and it's important to keep them in good working order to ensure safety and efficiency.

At KnK Industrial Contractors, we offer a full range of pressure vessel repairs and alteration services to keep your equipment operating at peak performance.

Our team of experts has extensive experience in pressure vessel repairs and alterations, and we use the latest technologies and techniques to ensure that each project is completed to the highest standards of quality and safety. Our services include:

Inspection & assessment: Our team of technicians will inspect your pressure vessels and assess their condition, identifying any issues that need to be addressed and making recommendations for repair or replacement.

Repairs: We can repair pressure vessels of all sizes and types, including vessels made of steel, stainless steel, and other materials. Our repair services include everything from simple welding and patching to complete overhauls, and we use only the highest-quality replacement parts and materials.

Alterations: If your pressure vessels need to be altered to meet changing requirements, our team of experts can help. We can modify or upgrade existing vessels, add new features or components, and more, to ensure that your pressure vessels are operating at peak performance.

Testing & Commissioning: After repairs or alterations, we will test and commission your pressure vessels to ensure that they are operating safely and efficiently, and we will provide a full report on their condition and performance.

At KnK Industrial Contractors, we are committed to providing safe, reliable, and efficient pressure vessel repairs and alteration services for the industrial sector.
