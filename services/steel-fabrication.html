Steel Fabrication

Steel fabrication is the process of creating structures, components, and equipment using steel as the primary material.

At KnK Industrial Contractors, we offer a full range of steel fabrication services, including:

Design and Engineering: Our experienced engineers and designers work with clients to create custom designs that meet their specific needs and requirements. We use the latest software and technologies to ensure that each design is accurate, efficient, and cost-effective.

Fabrication: We have a team of skilled steel fabricators who use the latest tools and techniques to create high-quality structures, components, and equipment. Our fabrication shop is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, allowing us to handle large and complex projects with ease.

Installation: Our team of experienced installers can handle all aspects of installation, from site preparation to final inspection. We work closely with clients to ensure that each installation is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality and safety standards.

Repair and Maintenance: In addition to new construction, we also offer repair and maintenance services to ensure that your steel fabrication remains in good working order and is ready to meet the demands of your business. Our team of experts can handle everything from routine maintenance to emergency repairs, and we use only the highest-quality replacement parts and materials.

At KnK Industrial Contractors, we are committed to providing safe, reliable, and efficient steel fabrication services for the industrial sector.
