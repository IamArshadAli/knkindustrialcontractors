Safety

Safety is our Top Priority

We understand that industrial construction projects can involve hazardous materials, heavy machinery, and other potential safety risks, and we take every precaution to ensure the safety of our workers and the public.

We have a comprehensive safety program that includes regular training, site inspections, strict protocols for hazardous materials, personal protective equipment, and state-of-the-art equipment and techniques.

We maintain a commitment to a safe and healthy work environment through regular safety meetings and ongoing training for employees

We believe that prioritizing safety is crucial for the success of their projects, allowing them to deliver high-quality facilities while minimizing risks and ensuring the well-being of everyone involved.

Our Internal Safety Program Includes:

Regular safety training for all employees, including training on the use of personal protective equipment and emergency response procedures.

Regular site inspections to identify and address potential hazards before they cause harm.

Implemention of fall protection measures, such as guardrails and safety harnesses, to prevent falls from heights.

Strict safety protocols for working with hazardous materials, such as chemicals and gases, to prevent exposure and accidental release.

Maintenance of all equipment and machineries to good working condition to prevent accidents and malfunctions.

Proper marking and securing the construction site to prevent unauthorized access and potential harm to workers and the public.

Compliance

KNK adheres to all Occupational Safety, Health Compliance associated with OSHA and our internal proprietary safety program.

Best Practices

This compliance assures our company members and clients that all business practices are performed in a safe manner, matching our corporate values and practices.
